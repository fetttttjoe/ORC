## Terms, in plain words

Commercial licenses are individually agreed and will always include two
things:

1. **Fair share** — a fair, proportionate compensation for the value orc
   contributes to what you monetize (flat fee, royalty, or revenue share,
   sized to the case; hobby-scale monetization gets hobby-scale terms).
2. **Recognition** — visible attribution: "Built on orc — © Yanneck
   Haendeler" (or equivalent) in your product's documentation, about screen,
   or credits.
